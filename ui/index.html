How to play
  • Connect your wallet, then tap Start Ranked Run.
  • You pay the entry fee to start. (Final amount is confirmed in your wallet popup.)
  • You get 5 minutes to play.
  • Move tiles with arrow keys, swipe, or tap buttons.
  • When you're done, tap Verify + Submit (or Quit and Submit).
  • Use the same wallet for both start and submit.
  • If your score becomes #1, you win half the pot. The other half stays for the next winner.
  • After a new #1 win, the next entry fee gets cut in half (but never below 1 ALPH).
  • If nobody gets a new #1 for 24h, the game resets on the next start.
